RSS Feed

My RSS feed is an free subscription so you don't miss any new information.
My recommendations are:

  • if you are on desktop:
    • Browser Extensions: Use tools like RSS Feed Reader or Feeder for Chrome and Firefox to get instant notifications.
    • Dedicated Apps: Try NetNewsWire (macOS) or Fluent Reader (Windows) for a clean, distraction-free reading experience.
    • Web-Based Aggregators: Use Feedly or Inoreader to sync your subscriptions across multiple devices.
  • if you are on mobile:
    • Reeder (iOS) or Readly (Android): These apps provide a smooth, gesture-based interface for catching up on the go.
    • NetNewsWire: A fantastic, open-source option for iPhone and iPad users that focuses on speed and privacy.
    • Feedly App: The best choise if you want to start reading on your computer and finish exactly where you left off on your phone.
